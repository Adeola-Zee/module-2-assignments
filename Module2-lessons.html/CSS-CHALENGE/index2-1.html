Colourful Creature

The giraffe is an African mammal.


It is the tallest living terrestrial animal and the largest ruminant on Earth.
The giraffe's distinguishing characteristics are;
its extremely long neck and legs, its horn-like ossicones and its spotted coat patterns.
Giraffes usually inhabit savannahs and woodlands.
They exist in scattered range extending from Chad in the north to South Africa in the south,
and from Niger in the west to Somalia in the east.
Their food source is leaves, fruits, and flowers of woody plants;
primarily acacia species which they browse at heights most other herbivores cannot reach.
